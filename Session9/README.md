# Session 9 — Workshop Synthesis & Next Steps

**Day 2 | Session 5 of 5**

## Overview

The final session brings it all together. You'll measure the platform's value using DORA metrics and KPI collectors, assess AI feature impact, and build your personal 30/60/90-day adoption roadmap. The goal: you leave with a clear plan for what to implement first, what to wait on, and how to prove value to leadership.

## What You'll Learn

- Measuring platform value: DORA metrics, deployment frequency, lead time, MTTR
- Presenting to leadership in business terms (hours saved, cost reduced)
- AI impact measurement: before/after comparison
- Value Stream Mapping and friction analysis
- Building a 30/60/90-day platform adoption roadmap
- Platform maturity re-assessment (compare with Session 1 baseline)

## Tools Required

- Python 3 (same setup from earlier sessions)

## Key Takeaway

Measure before you build, measure as you go, measure to prove value. Re-run the maturity assessment from Session 1 — your understanding of what "good" looks like has fundamentally changed. Present to leadership in business outcomes, not technical metrics.
